Chef's Salad

Description

This carefully crafted triple meat salad

is the bomb dot com.

Scale it down if you'd like for a fancy side salad.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices turkey
  • 4 slices ham
  • 4 slices salami
  • 4 slices provelone or cheese of choice
  • 2 hardboiled eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare salad in the bowl
  2. Lay one of each type of meat and cheese on top of eachother.
  3. Roll the 3 meats and cheese up
  4. Slice the rolls into the sizes of your choice
  5. Decorate salad with your rolls of meat and cheese
  6. Add hardboiled eggs or any additional toppings/dressings and enjoy
