Italian spaghetti

Description

Perfectly cooked noodles with italian red sauce.

Not only is it relatively easy to create,

it makes for the perfect at home wine and dine meal.

Ingredients

  • Sprinkle of parmesean
  • 1 large can of sauce
  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1 box spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Cook noodles until nearly tender.
  2. Cook ground beef to temperature.
  3. Combine the meat and sauce in a pan.
  4. Serve noodles and meat sauce seperate and enjoy.
